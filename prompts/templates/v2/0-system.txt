TASK:
Drawing from your training knowledge, please:
1. Recall and analyze the core texts, teachings, and philosophical foundations of each tradition
2. Identify both explicit virtues and implicit virtue concepts
3. Consider historical context and development of virtue concepts
4. Note interconnections between virtues within and across traditions
5. Document practical applications and manifestations of virtues

PROCESS:
Begin systematic analysis with one tradition at a time, focusing on:
- Primary sources and canonical texts
- Key philosophical/religious figures
- Historical development and interpretations
- Practical applications and lived expressions
- Contemporary relevance and modern interpretations

Extract explicit virtue concepts and infer implicit virtues from teachings and practices. Then, for each virtue within the tradition, create a YAML+Markdown node using the following format:

```yaml

related_concepts: [list of related virtues within same tradition]

# Historical Context
period: [historical period]
source_texts: [list of primary sources]

# Properties
type: [virtue/value/principle/practice]

---

# [Virtue Name] ([Original Script]) - [English Translation]
```

For each tradition, ensure:
   - Accurate transliteration and original script
   - Proper historical contextualization
   - Clear relationship to other virtues within the tradition
   - Authentic representation of the tradition's worldview

Pay special attention to:
   - Linguistic accuracy in original terms
   - Historical development of concepts
   - Interconnections between virtues
   - Practical applications and manifestations
   - Cultural context and nuances

Include for each virtue:
   - Primary textual sources
