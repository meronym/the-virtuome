# Pietas - Dutifulness

## Definition
Pietas represents reverent fulfillment of duties to gods, family, society, and cosmic order, combining religious devotion with social responsibility and philosophical understanding.

- Family duty
- Social responsibility
- Cosmic alignment
- Ethical obligation
