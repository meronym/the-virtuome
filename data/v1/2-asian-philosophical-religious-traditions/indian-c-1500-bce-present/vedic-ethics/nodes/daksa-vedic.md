# Dakṣa (दक्ष) - Skill/Competence

## Definition
Dakṣa represents skillful competence and mastery in both practical and spiritual domains, combining technical excellence with sacred purpose.

## Key Aspects
- Practical skill
- Ritual expertise
- Technical mastery
- Efficient action
- Sacred competence

## Historical Development
Originally emphasized in ritual context, expanded to encompass general competence in all worthy endeavors.

## Contemporary Relevance
Applicable to professional excellence, craftsmanship, and skilled practice in any field.

## Notable Quotes
> "Through Dakṣa comes mastery." - Vedic teaching
> "Skill in action is yoga." - Bhagavad Gītā (later development)

## Related Practices
- Ritual performance
- Skill development
- Craft mastery
- Professional excellence
- Technical precision
