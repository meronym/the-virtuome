# Ukama - Universal Relationality

## Definition
The recognition and maintenance of relationships between humans, nature, ancestors, and the spiritual realm; cosmic interconnectedness.

## Key Aspects
- Environmental stewardship
- Ancestral connection
- Spiritual awareness
- Natural harmony
- Social bonds

## Historical Development
Emerged from traditional African understanding of cosmic order and human place within it. Developed through environmental practices and spiritual ceremonies.

## Contemporary Relevance
Informs African environmental ethics and sustainable development approaches. Valuable for addressing ecological crises and social alienation.

## Notable Quotes
> "The dead are not dead, they are in the rustling trees." - African proverb
> "To care for nature is to care for ourselves." - Traditional saying
