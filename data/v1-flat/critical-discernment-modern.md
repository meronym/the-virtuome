# Critical Discernment - Ethical Judgment

## Definition
The virtue of making well-reasoned judgments about truth, value, and action, combining intellectual rigor with ethical awareness.

## Key Aspects

- Information evaluation
- Ethical assessment
- Context awareness
- Balanced judgment
